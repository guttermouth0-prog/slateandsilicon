First principle

The goal is not to become anti-technology. The goal is to stop letting technology decide the shape of your attention.

Orientation

Your focus did not vanish. It adapted to a system that rewards interruption.

The Path

Three moves. No theatrics.

01 / Understand

Name the system.

Before you can change your behavior, you need to see what is shaping it: feeds, defaults, notification loops, and frictionless checking.

02 / Interrupt

Break the pattern.

Small constraints create space. Turn down inputs, add friction, and make distraction slightly less automatic.

03 / Rebuild

Recover depth.

Use analog rituals, defined work blocks, and low-noise routines to rebuild your tolerance for silence, boredom, and focus.
