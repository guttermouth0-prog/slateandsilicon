THE DETOX PLANNER

7-DAY ANALOG PROTOCOL

You’ve spent enough time online to feel the shift.

Attention doesn’t land the way it used to.

Not broken. Just… dispersed.

This isn’t a fix.

It’s a controlled environment.

Seven days where your attention operates under different conditions.

Each day is reduced to three things:

Initialization — no-screen morning
The One Move — single-task depth
Decommission — system shutdown

Alongside this, small experiments:

removing color
removing notifications
removing constant access

Not permanently.

Just long enough to see what changes.

Most people don’t need more discipline.

They need a different system.
